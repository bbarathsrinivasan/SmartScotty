## ğŸ’¡ Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific**: "Show me assignments for 10-601" is better than "show assignments"
2. **Use course codes**: "10-601" or "15-445" work well
3. **Mention timeframes**: "this week", "next 7 days", "today"
4. **Specify types**: "exams", "quizzes", "projects", "homework"
5. **Ask for summaries**: "Give me an overview" for quick insights
