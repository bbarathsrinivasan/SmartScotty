## üí° Tips for Best Results

1. **Be specific with course codes**: Use "10-601" or "15-445" for best results
2. **Mention what you need**: "syllabus", "schedule", "office hours", etc.
3. **Ask for specific weeks**: "week 3", "week 5" for schedule details
4. **Combine queries**: Ask for multiple types of information in one query
5. **Use natural language**: The AI understands various phrasings
